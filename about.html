Our Mission

Turning Curiosity Into Unforgettable Journeys

GeoQuest is a travel discovery platform built for curious explorers who want more from their journeys. We go beyond the tourist trail — surfacing hidden destinations, local experiences, and personalised travel insights that help you plan trips with confidence and intention.

Whether you're mapping out your next adventure or simply dreaming of where to go, GeoQuest brings the world closer. Our platform combines smart discovery tools with curated travel content, making it easier than ever to find places that truly match who you are and how you travel.

Our Journey

How GeoQuest Came to Be

From a small idea in Oxford to a platform trusted by travellers in 120 countries — here's our story.

2018

The Idea is Born

Unique, Pratik, and Anusha meet at a travel conference in London and decide that trip planning deserves something far smarter than a search engine.

2019

Beta Launch

GeoQuest v1 launches with 5 curated destinations and an early community of 150 beta testers. Feedback shapes everything.

2021

AI Engine Launch

Pratik leads the team and GeoQuest's destination-matching AI goes live.

2023

20,000 Explorers

GeoQuest reaches 19,000 active users across 10 countries. The platform expands to include user-generated reviews and local guides.

2025

Global Recognition

Named one of the "Top 10 Travel Innovation Platforms" by Travel Weekly. Operations expanded to 10+ countries with a team of 20.
